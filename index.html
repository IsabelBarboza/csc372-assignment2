Dine On Campus

Student bites, quick eats & bold flavors around UNCG.

Welcome Food Lovers

Life as a UNCG student revolves around deadlines, caffeine, and good food, and this blog highlights a few tasty spots near the Petty Building where student can recharge.

All the locations featured here are within walking distance, making them perfect between classes.

From saucy wings to speedy sandwiches, these places bring flavor and convenience together

Grab a friend, take a break, and treat yourself.

East Coast Wings + Grill

A favorite hangout for UNCG students who love bold flavors and casual vibes. This spot is known for its variety of wing sauces, combo meals, and relaxed environment. Students can even enjoy a 10% discount, which makes it perfect for budget-friendly cravings after class.
Hours: Mon–Thu: 11:00 AM – 10:00 PM

Honey BBQ Wings

Sweet, smoky, and dangerously addictive. A crowd favorite for a reason.

Classic Buffalo Wings

Spicy, saucy, and full of attitude; the kind of wings that wake up your taste buds.

Chicken Tender Basket

Crispy comfort food served hot with fries. Simple, classic, delicioso.

Jimmy John's

Known for speed and consistency, Jimmy John's is the go-to for students who need a quick bite before class. Sandwiches are made fresh, simple, and satisfying. Perfect when time is short but hunger is real.
Hours: Mon–Fri: 10:30 AM – 9:00 PM

Turkey Sandwich

Fresh turkey, crisp veggies, and soft bread. A classic that never disappoints.

Veggie Sandwich

Light, fresh, and crunchy. Great option when you want something simple but tasty.

Italian Sandwich

Loaded with meats and flavor; bold, hearty, and perfect after a long day.

Chick-fil-A

Located right on the UNCG campus, Chick-fil-A is a top choice for students needing something quick, familiar, and satisfying. Known for its friendly service and consistent quality, it's a reliable stop between classes or before study sessions. The menu is simple but packed with flavor and comfort-food favorites.
Hours: Mon–Fri: 7:30 AM – 8:00 PM

Chicken Sandwich

Juicy chicken, toasted bun, and that signature flavor everyone loves. Perfectly seasoned, served with fresh pickles, and a soft, buttery bun that holds it all together.

Market Salad

A fresh and filling salad with grilled chicken, mixed greens, fruit, cheese, and nuts.

Chicken Nuggets

Perfect bite-sized pieces. Crispy, tender, and dangerously addictive.

Tips

East Coast Wings offers a student discount, which makes it a top pick for group hangouts.

Chick-Fil-A and Jimmy John’s are lifesavers between classes when time is tight but hunger hits hard.
